How We Work

Finding Solutions For Your Business
From the moment you connect with us to the thrill of seeing your brand come to life, our process is your assurance of excellence. With us, it’s not just business; it’s a partnership built on trust and results.
Learn how we will
work together
01.
Inquiry Submission
To begin a potential collaboration, submit an inquiry via our contact form. You’ll be contacted by our team within one or two business days. We only proceed to next step with direct access and conversation with key decision makers.
02.
Discovery Meeting
You will be invited to our free consultation session also known as discovery meeting. This meeting aims to discuss your business situation, evaluate our potential contribution, and plan a future meeting with key decision-makers.
03.
Conversation With Leadership
An essential step before proceeding to a proposal is having an open conversation with c-level decision makers, typically a founder and/or CEO and other folks in your steering group. Understanding the wider business context from their vantage point is vital to our process. This meeting allows both parties to ask questions, assess chemistry, and gain a deep understanding of your ambitions and objectives.
04.
Project Scope
Uncon offers serveral different services and packages, each tailored to different brand challenges and proven effective across various industries and complexities. We’ll align your needs with what we can offer to build our proposal around.
05.
Proposal Development
Following successful discussions with your leadership team and a confirmed fit, we create a straightforward proposal to outline the scope of work, fees, and project timeline. We’ll set up a call to discuss it with your decision maker.
06.
Proposal Acceptance & Onboarding
Upon accepting our proposal, we issue a Service Agreement or Contract and request the initial deposit to start the project. Our team then begins a formal client onboarding to set us all up for success.
